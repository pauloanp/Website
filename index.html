About
If my life was a storm, photography would be like a gust of wind, more like a breeze. It has only been in my life for a short period of time, yet it is one, if not my favourite way to express myself. It's an art form which has become quickly a passion. I studied Arts at Francisco Franco highschool, having always been keen on drawing and arts in general. It was at the beggining of my second year on the course that I started to enjoy photography more, although from my smartphone. Nearing the end of my last year, and after being inspired by many artists and photographers alike, I bought my first DSLR, which is my current camera. I haven't stopped ever since. I am currently studying Graphic Design at ESAC.CR. My artist side has ever more increased in my, and photography is now embedded into me.

My work is ever changing and in constant evolution, and I'm always searching for new grounds to walk upon.
